A person who inspires me every day.

Who is Tim Berners-Lee?

The first version of HTML was written by Tim Berners-Lee in 1993. Since then, there have been many different versions of HTML. The most widely used version throughout the 2000's was HTML 4.01, which became an official standard in December 1999.

Notable Achievements

  • 1994: Became one of only six members of the World Wide Web Hall of Fame
  • 1997: Appointed an Officer of the Order of the British Empire (OBE) "for services to global computer networking".
  • 2003: Received the Computer History Museum's Fellow Award, for his seminal contributions to the development of the World Wide Web.
  • 2022: Seoul Peace Prize : For inventing the World Wide Web, supporting policies to address unequal Internet access, and aiming to decentralize user data with the Solid project.

Inspiring Quote

"When something is such a creative medium as the web, the limits to it are our imagination"
